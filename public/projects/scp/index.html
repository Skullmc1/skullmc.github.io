ABOUT THE FOUNDATION

Our Mission

The SCP Foundation is tasked with containing anomalous objects, entities, and phenomena. These anomalies pose a significant threat to global security by threatening either physical or psychological harm. The Foundation operates to maintain normalcy, so that the worldwide civilian population can live and work without fear of the supernatural, the extraterrestrial, and other unexplainable incidents.

Object Classifications

  • Safe: Easily contained anomalies
  • Euclid: Anomalies requiring more resources to contain
  • Keter: Extremely difficult or impossible to fully contain
  • Thaumiel: Anomalies used to contain other SCPs
  • Neutralized: Anomalies that are no longer anomalous

Core Protocols

  • Secure: Locating and containing anomalies
  • Contain: Keeping anomalies hidden from the public
  • Protect: Safeguarding humanity from anomalous effects

Security Clearance

Information is strictly regulated on a need-to-know basis. Clearance levels range from Level 1 to Level 5, with additional specialized clearances for specific anomalies. Unauthorized access or information sharing is grounds for immediate termination.
