Thaumiel Classification

Thaumiel-class SCPs are highly classified anomalies used by the Foundation to contain or counteract other SCPs or anomalous phenomena. The mere existence of this class is known to few Foundation personnel outside the O5 Council.

HIGHEST CLEARANCE REQUIRED: Access to Thaumiel-class documentation requires Level 5 clearance or O5 Council authorization.
