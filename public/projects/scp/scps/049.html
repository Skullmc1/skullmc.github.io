BIOHAZARD LEVEL 3

SCP-049

Object Class: Euclid
CAUTION: LETHAL TOUCH • BIOLOGICAL HAZARD • COGNITOHAZARD RISK

Special Containment Procedures

SCP-049 is to be contained in a Standard Secure Humanoid Containment Cell in Research Sector-02 at Site-19. SCP-049 must be sedated before any attempts to transport it. During transport, SCP-049 must be secured within a Class III Humanoid Restriction Harness and monitored by no fewer than two armed guards.

While SCP-049 is generally cooperative with most Foundation personnel, outbursts may occur at any time without warning. During these outbursts, the entity will attempt to cure what it perceives as "the disease."

Description

SCP-049 is a humanoid entity, roughly 1.9 meters in height, which bears the appearance of a medieval plague doctor. While SCP-049 appears to be wearing the thick robes and ceramic mask indicative of that profession, the garments instead seem to be part of the entity's physical form.

SCP-049 is capable of causing immediate death through physical contact. Postmortem examination of subjects killed by SCP-049 typically show no cause of death. SCP-049 then performs a surgery on the deceased subject, often resulting in the creation of instances of SCP-049-2.

Interview Log 049-1

Dr. Sherman: "What is this cure you speak of?"
SCP-049: "The cure for what ails us all, doctor. The Great Pestilence that haunts the air and corrupts the body. I sense it even now, moving about the room, running through your veins..."
Dr. Sherman: "Can you describe this Pestilence?"
SCP-049: "It is... quite impossible to describe in such limited terms. But I know it when I see it. And I see it all around."

Addendum 049.1

On [REDACTED], SCP-049 began to refuse most interviews, stating that it "had work to do." The entity has since displayed increased interest in deceased bodies, and appears to have made several attempts to improve its surgical methods.

- Dr. [REDACTED]
