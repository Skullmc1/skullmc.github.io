MAXIMUM SECURITY CONTAINMENT

SCP-682

Object Class: Keter
EXTREME DANGER • HOSTILE ENTITY • REGENERATIVE PROPERTIES

Special Containment Procedures

SCP-682 must be contained in a 5 m x 5 m x 5 m chamber with 25 cm reinforced acid-resistant steel plate lining all inside surfaces. The containment chamber should be filled with hydrochloric acid until SCP-682 is submerged and incapacitated.

Any attempts to destroy SCP-682 must be approved by no less than O5-level personnel. Due to its frequent attempts to breach containment, destruction of SCP-682 is considered a top priority.

Description

SCP-682 is a large, vaguely reptilian creature of unknown origin. It appears to be extremely intelligent and has displayed highly aggressive behavior. SCP-682 appears to hate all life, which it expresses through violent attacks and caustic verbal communications in English.

SCP-682 has demonstrated extreme physical resilience, adaptability, and regenerative capabilities. It has survived multiple termination attempts using physical, chemical, and nuclear methods. The creature's regenerative properties allow it to reconstruct its body mass even from severe damage.

Termination Log 682-E11

██/██/████

Termination Attempt: Exposure to SCP-409

Result: Failure. SCP-682 adapted crystalline properties temporarily before violently shedding them. Three personnel lost.

██/██/████

Termination Attempt: High-energy plasma discharge

Result: Failure. SCP-682 absorbed energy and increased in mass. Containment breach occurred.

Interview Log 682-E12

Dr. ████: "Why do you hate all life?"
SCP-682: "You are disgusting..."
Dr. ████: "Many of the researchers here have hypothesized about your origins."
SCP-682: "[EXPLETIVES REDACTED] You are all diseased vermin. I will rid this world of your filth."

Addendum 682-B

In the event of a containment breach, Site-██ is to be evacuated immediately. Mobile Task Force Omega-7 ("Red Right Hand") must be deployed for recontainment. Use of nuclear weapons has been authorized in the event of a full breach scenario.

- O5-█
