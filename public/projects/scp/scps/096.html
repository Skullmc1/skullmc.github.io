SCP-096

Object Class: Euclid
WARNING: VIEWING SCP-096's FACE TRIGGERS A RESPONSE EVENT

Special Containment Procedures

SCP-096 is to be contained in its cell, a 5 m x 5 m x 5 m airtight steel cube, at all times. Weekly checks for any cracks or holes are mandatory. There are to be absolutely no video surveillance or optical tools of any kind inside SCP-096's cell. Security personnel will use pre-installed pressure sensors and laser detectors to ensure SCP-096's presence inside the cell.

Any and all photos, video, or recordings of SCP-096's likeness are strictly forbidden without approval from Dr. ███ and O5-█.

Description

SCP-096 is a humanoid creature measuring approximately 2.38 meters in height. Subject shows very little muscle mass, with preliminary analysis of body mass suggesting mild malnutrition. Arms are grossly out of proportion with the rest of the subject's body, with an approximate length of 1.5 meters each. Skin is mostly devoid of pigmentation, with no sign of any body hair.

SCP-096's jaw can open to four (4) times the normal human maximum. Other facial features remain similar to an average human, with the exception of the eyes, which are also devoid of pigmentation. It is not yet known whether SCP-096 is blind or not.

Note: SCP-096 is normally extremely docile, with pressure sensors inside its cell indicating it spends most of its day pacing by the eastern wall. However, when someone views SCP-096's face, whether it be directly, via video recording, or even a photograph, it will enter a stage of considerable emotional distress.

Incident 096-1-A

Date: [REDACTED]

During a routine cell transfer, D-9341 accidentally viewed SCP-096's face for approximately 0.09 seconds. SCP-096 immediately entered an agitated emotional state and [DATA EXPUNGED]. Time from breach to termination of D-9341: 27 minutes.

- Dr. [REDACTED]

Addendum 096-1

Due to the possibility of a mass-casualty event should SCP-096's face be captured by digital or video equipment, Dr. ███ has proposed immediate termination of SCP-096. Proposal is currently under review by O5 Command.
