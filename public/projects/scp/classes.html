KETER CLASS

Keter-class SCPs are anomalies that are exceedingly difficult to contain consistently or reliably, with containment procedures often being extensive and complex.

  • Highly dangerous or unpredictable
  • Resource-intensive containment
  • High breach potential
View Keter SCPs

EUCLID CLASS

Euclid-class SCPs are anomalies that require more resources to contain completely or where containment isn't always reliable.

  • Unpredictable behavior
  • Moderate containment difficulty
  • Partial understanding
View Euclid SCPs

SAFE CLASS

Safe-class SCPs are anomalies that are easily and reliably contained. This does not mean they are not dangerous, only that they are easy to contain.

  • Reliable containment
  • Well understood
  • Predictable behavior
View Safe SCPs

THAUMIEL CLASS

Thaumiel-class SCPs are anomalies that the Foundation specifically uses to contain other SCPs. They are among the most closely guarded Foundation resources.

  • Used for containment
  • Highly classified
  • Limited access
