Where Reality Fades Away...

Level 0: The Lobby

Welcome to the beginning of your nightmare. The buzzing of fluorescent lights fills your ears, a constant, maddening hum that never ceases. The wallpaper, a sickly yellow reminiscent of jaundiced skin, seems to stretch endlessly in all directions. The air is thick with the smell of damp carpet and mold.

The moist carpet squelches beneath your feet as you wander through this labyrinth of monotony. Every corner reveals another identical hallway, every turn leads to the same yellow walls. The fluorescent lights above flicker in an endless dance, casting moving shadows that seem to follow your every step.

Level 1: The Warehouse

An expansive warehouse stretches into darkness. Towering metal shelves create a maze-like structure, their contents obscured by shadows. The concrete floor is stained with unknown substances, and the air carries a metallic tang. The ceiling disappears into darkness above, occasionally broken by dim, swinging lights.

The distant sound of machinery echoes through the vast space, though no active equipment is visible. Rusted forklifts and broken pallets create obstacles in your path. Between the shelves, shadows seem to move with purpose, always just at the edge of your vision. The air grows colder as you venture deeper.

The loading dock area stretches before you, an eternal twilight zone where mist curls around abandoned shipping containers. The roll-up doors are frozen in various states of decay, their metal surfaces corroded into abstract patterns. The fog seems to pulse with its own rhythm.

Level 2: The Pipe Dreams

A maze of narrow maintenance tunnels stretches in all directions. The walls are lined with pipes of varying sizes, some vibrating with unknown pressures within. Steam hisses from joints and valves, creating an ever-present mist that limits visibility. The heat is oppressive, and condensation drips from every surface.

The boiler room looms before you, massive tanks and machinery casting strange shadows in the red emergency lighting. The temperature here is nearly unbearable, and the constant drumming of machinery creates a disorienting rhythm. Warning signs, their text long faded, cover the walls.

At the junction, valve wheels of various sizes cover the walls like mechanical spiderwebs. Some spin slowly on their own, while others are frozen with rust. The pipes here form impossible geometries, seeming to bend in ways that defy physics.

Level 3: The Electrical Station

Banks of electrical panels line the walls, their surfaces covered in gauges, switches, and blinking lights. The air crackles with static electricity, making your hair stand on end. Bundles of cables snake across the ceiling like mechanical vines, disappearing into dark conduits.

Massive transformers hum with barely contained power, occasional arcs of electricity jumping between them. The air here is charged with ozone, and the constant electrical buzz seems to resonate in your teeth. Warning signs about high voltage cast their reflections in pools of leaked oil.

The control room is a maze of vintage equipment and flickering monitors. Buttons and switches cover every surface, some blinking in patterns that seem almost meaningful. The screens display impossible readings and error messages in languages that don't exist.

Level 4: The Office Space

An endless maze of cubicles stretches to the horizon. The carpet is a dizzying pattern of blues and grays, and the walls are covered in faded motivational posters. Empty chairs face blank monitors, some still displaying ancient screensavers. The fluorescent lights above cast a sickly glow that never seems to reach the floor properly.

The break room appears frozen in time, with vintage vending machines humming softly in the corner. The coffee maker endlessly drips into a pot that never fills, and the clock on the wall spins randomly. Chairs are arranged around tables as if their occupants just left, but the layer of dust suggests otherwise.

The conference room stands ready for a meeting that will never happen. The projector casts strange shadows on the screen, displaying presentations filled with impossible graphs and incomprehensible data. The whiteboard is covered in equations that seem to shift when you're not looking directly at them.
