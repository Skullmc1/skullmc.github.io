About Lethal Cooperation

At Lethal Cooperation, we are dedicated to collecting scrap from various moons and planets, preserving celestial bodies from junk accumulation while generating meaningful employment opportunities. Our mission is to create a sustainable future for both our galaxy and its inhabitants.

Our Services

Spacecraft Scrapping

Deploying advanced spacecraft to collect and process space debris efficiently.

Recycling Operations

Transforming collected scrap into reusable materials for various industries.

Job Creation

Providing employment opportunities across different sectors within our operations.

How It Works

1. Collection

Our spacecraft identify and gather scrap from designated celestial bodies.

2. Processing

The collected materials are transported to our processing facilities for recycling.

3. Employment

We create jobs by managing operations, maintenance, and expanding our services.
